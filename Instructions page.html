How To Use This Awesome App:

To record a run

Step 1: Click on the "START" button to begin recording your run.

Step 2: Place your device anywhere on your torso for the best and most accurate reading.

Step 3: Start running! =)

To save a run

Step 1: Click on the "STOP" button to stop recording your run.

Step 2: Click on the SAVE button to save your route.

Step 3: Enter a name for your route. Your run is now saved!

To view a run

Step 1: In your record page, tap on the three dots icon on the top right of your screen.

Step 2: A drop down menu would apprear, select "Routes".

Step 3: You would be brought to a page with a map and some details about a route. Tap on the three dots icon on the top right of your screen.

Step 4: A drop down menu would apprear with the name(s) of the routes that you have saved, select your desired route.
